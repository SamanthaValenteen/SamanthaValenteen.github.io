Miscellaneous Small Projects

I worked with a team at the BTU Lab to create an entry for a pumpkin carving contest hosted by the engineering program.

Our entry featured three tentacles attached to servos inside the pumpkin that would listlessly wave around and increase when someone stodd in front of it (using its proximity sensor cyborg eye).

The Arduino controlling everything was hidden within the severed head. You can see the result in this short video. Our pumpkin took second place in both Scariest and Best Overall!
