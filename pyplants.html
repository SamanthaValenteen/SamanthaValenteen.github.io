PyPlants

In a combined effort to both teach myself about the Flask framework and to keep my houseplants alive, I created a simple web application that keeps track of my plants, when they were last watered, and when they should be watered again.

It's a project in progress. Future efforts will go towards having the application send text message alert reminders and updating watering date based on the response.
